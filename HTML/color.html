Color Palette

The Pacific Ocean served as our primary inspiration for the PocketGig mobile app's color palette, reflecting the essence of Vancouver's coastal lifestyle.

The Ocean Blue represents reliability and trust, mirroring the vast, steady waters that surround the city. The vibrant Teal Blue captures the dynamic energy of ocean waves, symbolizing opportunity â€” key aspect of the gig economy. Finally, the bright Aqua Blue brings in a sense of freshness and innovation, much like the open ocean inviting endless possibilities.

Together, these colors create a clean, modern, and inviting aesthetic, aligning with our mission to make gig work seamless and empowering for Vancouverites.
